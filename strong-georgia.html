STRONG GEORGIA

2024, ART PANEL FOR DEMOCRACY

IN OCTOBER 2024, AN ART PANEL WAS CREATED − "STRONG GEORGIA", IN WHICH MANY ARTISTS PARTICIPATED. THE PANEL SERVES TO DEEPEN EUROPEAN VALUES ​​AND DEMOCRATIC PROCESSES IN GEORGIA.

Each artist presented their own original vision and approach to the modern country. One of the works is by Tamar Gedevanishvili.

"In this work, I wanted to show a free, strong woman in an independent and free Georgia."

Tamar Gedevanishvili
