Group Exhibition for #Elene

2025, Collect Art Gallery

On May 14, 2025, a charity group exhibition-sale was held for #Elene, organized by Collect Art Gallery and N9 Gallery. Elene was seriously injured while snowboarding and had to undergo a rehabilitation course abroad, for which she needed a substantial amount of money.

Collect Art Gallery gallerists Maka Gotsiridze and Tamar Khelashvili, as well as N9 Gallery gallerist Maka Iovadze, the author of the idea of ​​the event Keti Batiashvili herself and the artists decided to organize a charity group exhibition-sale and use the proceeds from the sold works to help the young person.

Many artists and visitors took part in the exhibition. The gallery presented works by artists in various media, including graphics, paintings, prints, and textiles.

Several works by Tamar Gedevanishvili were also presented at the exhibition. Both paintings and graphic works. The proceeds from the sold works were transferred entirely to a special account allocated for Elene. Everyone hopes that with joint efforts, it will be possible for Elene to return to a healthy and fulfilling life.
