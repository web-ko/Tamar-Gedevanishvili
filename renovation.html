RENOVATION

DAVID KAKABADZE KUTAISI FINE ART GALLERY – 19 APRIL–10 MAY, 2024

2024, 19 April–10 May – group exhibition of modern Georgian Artists in Kutaisi at David Kakabadze Fine Art Gallery. Curators Eliso Chogovadze, Mamia Tkeshelashvili. Were presented two works – 1. graphic from the series “For a Whole Life“ 21x30 cm (paper, pen), 2. From the series “Improper Intersections”, 80x60 cm (paper, acrylic)

Participate Artists: Oleg Timchenko, Niko Tsetskhladze, Murtaz Shvelidze, Zura Gikashvili, Tamar Gedevanishvili, Mamia tkeshelashvili, kote jincharadze, Mamuka Tsetskhladze, Tina Tskhadadze, Lia Shvelidze, Tamuna Melikishvili, Davit Popiashvili, Maka Jishkariani, Rita Khachaturian, Mamuka Tsutskiridze, Guram Khetsuriani, Lia Ukleba, Giorgi Tandashvili, Beka Bolkvadze, David Aleksidze, Tamuna Kobiashvili, Gogi Khakhutashvili, Aleksandre Acharadze, Levan Gogitidze, Mirian Chikhradze, Kakhaber Demuradze, Liana Nakashidze, Lado Gorgodze

"RENOVATION " is dedicated to the renewal of this gallery and the appearance of new faces. Each artist presented his original vision and approach to the modern world. The works of artists from Batumi, Kutaisi and Tbilisi gave new life to Davit Kakabadze Art Gallery in Kutaisi.

Two works of Tamar Gedevanishvili were presented here -1. graphic from the series "For a Whole Life" 21x30 cm (paper, pen), 2. From the series "Improper intersections", 80x60 cm (paper, acrylic)
