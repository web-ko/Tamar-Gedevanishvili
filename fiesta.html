FIESTA

JOINT EXHIBITION DECEMBER 19 - JANUARY 14, 2023

The word "fiesta" already evokes some pleasant excitement... The word "miracle" in literature means an important action. Each of us has an individual perception of the holiday. Mood, color, or condition... Therefore, this joint exhibition is interesting because our extraordinary artists will give us a glimpse of the mood and perception they have towards the holiday in different mediums. Let's create a holiday together!"

Maka Iovadze, Gallerist,

Owner of N9Gallery

The joint exhibition, "Fiesta," debuted at N9Gallery on December 19, 2023. Tamar Gedevanishvili showcased three pieces from the "Improper Intersections" series. One work, a 4-part panel measuring 200x140 cm, paper, acrylic, the other two measured 42x30 cm each.

Participating artists: Tamar Gedevanishvili, Aleksandre Varvaridze, Mamuka Tshekladze, Lia Shvelidze, Levan Chichinadze, Tamuna Melikishvili, Levan Choghoshvili, Giorgi Dundua, Gela Patiashvili, Maka Jishkariani, Maka Gotsiridze, Kakhi Eradze, Keti Shalamberidze, Lali Kutateladze, Keti Batiashvili, Davit Melikishvili, Mamia Malazonia, Teona Paichadze, Anka Kalatozishvili, Nino Chitaishvili.

"I believe today's exhibition resonates with the perpetual holiday people carry within. It can be anything, etched vividly in the mind, alive when time is powerless. It's not forgotten; it's ever-present, an inseparable celebration."

Tamar Gedevanishvili
