CONNECTIONS

PERFORMANCE: JOINT EXHIBITION MAY 6, 2023, EXPO GEORGIA

In celebration of European Union Day, an art performance titled "Connections" took place at Expo Georgia. This event was held as part of European Day, generously supported by the European Union and the United Nations Development Program, with curation by Khatuna Khabuliani.

Sixteen artists collaborated to create a significant polyptych. This work, both in content and composition, converges diverse perspectives on human existence and rights onto one cohesive canvas.

When words fall short, art speaks. The polyptych, crafted by Georgian artists, symbolizes Georgia's commitment to European values, equality, and human rights.

The distinctive blue hue of the European Union harmonizes with the installation "Connections," a creation by renowned Georgian artists, marking Europe Day.

Participating artists: Tamar Gedevanishvili, Gega Kutateli, Davit Kukhalashvili, Tamar Khmiadashvili, Murtaz Shvelidze, Lia Shvelidze, Davit Aleksidze, Giorgi Getiashvili, Archibald Kordzaia, Giorgi Otiashvili, Avtandil Ghakhokidze, Kote Jincharadze, Tedo Rekhviashvili, Kote Sulaberidze, Manuchar Okrostsvaridze, Tamuna Melikishvili.

Supporters: European Union and the United Nations Development Program, as part of the Human Rights project.
